Who's Mariam

Mariam is what we'd call a fashion guru these days. She spends a lot of time browsing and hunting on online clothing platforms to find the perfect addition to her latest outfit. She was raised in a modest family with a mum who graduated from fashion school. Her mother never had the chance to live her designer dream, but she was able to indulge this in Mariam throughout her childhood. Since she was young, Mariam has been drawn to all kinds of colours, patterns and eye-catching designs for her outfits.

What's in her Wardrobe?

Recently, Mariam has been particularly drawn to vintage designer collections from the early 2000s. She was particularly taken with the collections from Roberto Cavalli and John Galliano, two of the most influential designers of the early 2000s. Meanwhile, she's picked up lots of different items from various collections. She's on the lookout for a few key pieces from different collections and putting them together in what she calls a "set". These sets feature stand-out tops, jeans, skirts, dresses, cardigans, handbags and matching shoes. Her collection has been growing over the past couple of years, and she now has some of the most iconic pieces every fashion girl would have dreamed of. Upcomming Plans?

"All these clothes look immaculate, but at what cost" would you ask me. Sometimes, her passion for clothes ends up being the biggest expense in her monthly budget. However, she's been saving up in the hope of being able to afford a ticket for the next fashion week. It's always been her dream to walk the catwalk herself and show off clothes designed by her favourite contemporary fashion artists.
